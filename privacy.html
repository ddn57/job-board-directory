Data Transparency & Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. JavaScript Tracking

We use JavaScript to provide core features such as the search bar and category filtering. This interaction is processed locally in your browser to provide immediate results. Search terms are not stored on our servers.

2. Local Storage Usage

Our site uses Local Storage to remember your settings. This data remains on your device and includes:

  • theme: Remembers your Light or Dark mode preference.
  • privacyChoice: Remembers if you clicked "Accept" or "Reject" on our privacy banner.

3. Google Analytics

If you click Accept on our consent banner, we load Google Analytics to collect anonymous traffic data (e.g., number of visitors, most clicked job boards). If you click Reject, the Google Analytics script is never loaded and no tracking occurs.

4. External Links

We provide links to dozens of external job boards. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those external sites once you leave our domain.
