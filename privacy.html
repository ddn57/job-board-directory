Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Job Board Directory ("we", "our", or "us") handles your data using JavaScript, Local Storage, and Analytics.

1. JavaScript & Functional Interaction

We use JavaScript to provide core features. This includes the search bar and category filtering. This processing happens locally in your browser and terms are not sent to our servers.

2. Local Storage

We use Local Storage to remember your settings. This data stays on your device:

  • theme: Remembers your choice of Light or Dark mode.
  • privacyChoice: Remembers if you clicked "Accept" or "Reject" on our banner.

3. Google Analytics

If you click "Accept" on our banner, we use Google Analytics to collect anonymous traffic data. If you click "Reject," this tracking is disabled.

4. External Links

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external job boards linked in this directory.
