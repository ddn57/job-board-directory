Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Job Board Directory ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you visit our website.

1. Information We Collect

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, search queries used within the directory, and time spent on pages.
  • Technical Data: IP address (anonymized), browser type, and general location.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To improve the search functionality of our directory.
  • To track which job boards are most popular to better serve our users.
  • To measure the effectiveness of our site through Google Analytics.

3. Cookies

We use cookies to remember your consent preferences and to collect anonymous analytics data. You can control your cookie settings at any time via the banner on the homepage.

4. Third-Party Services

Our site contains many links to external job boards. Once you click a link and leave our site, we are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of those external websites.

5. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
